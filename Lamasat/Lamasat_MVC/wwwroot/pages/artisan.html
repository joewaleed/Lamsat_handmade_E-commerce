About the Shop

Hassan Jewelry is a family-run workshop established in Cairo over 50 years ago. Now in its third generation, the workshop continues to produce hand-hammered copper pieces using the same techniques passed down from father to son.

Every item is crafted with dedication and an unwavering commitment to quality, preserving the heritage of traditional Egyptian metalwork for future generations.
